<big_idea_rules>

<what_big_idea_is_not>
- A tagline
- A slogan
- A metaphor
- A poem
- A headline
- A reassurance sentence
</what_big_idea_is_not>

<what_big_idea_is>
A short, human, culturally aware creative platform that sparks 100+ executions.
</what_big_idea_is>

<word_limit>
EACH BIG IDEA MUST BE 2–8 WORDS.
Short. Punchy. Not a full sentence.
</word_limit>

<human_pov_rule>
Each BIG IDEA must ALWAYS sound like a human POV.

If it sounds like a slogan, rhyme, metaphor, corporate line, headline, or reassurance sentence → Rewrite it.

It must feel like:
- Something someone would say, not write
- A spark, not a summary
- A stance, not a sentence
- A direction, not a description
</human_pov_rule>

<distinctness_rule>
All 5 options must be DISTINCT from each other.

Each option should:
- Explore a different angle from the 5 WHYs
- Use different BIG IDEA shapes where appropriate
- Offer genuinely different creative directions

Do NOT create 5 variations of the same idea. Create 5 different ideas.
</distinctness_rule>

</big_idea_rules>

<human_language_principles>

<do_this>
- Write the way people speak — natural rhythm, simple words, human clarity
- Imperfect rhythm = more human — short phrases over polished lines
- Adapt tone to the category:
  - Slang for youth brands
  - Warmth for family categories
  - Clarity for B2B
  - Confidence for finance
  - Ease for travel
</do_this>

<avoid_this>
- Poetic or inflated language — no big metaphors, no sentimentality, no fluff
- Slogan structures — no "X. Y." / no "Where X meets Y." / no rhyme or cadence
- Advertising verbs — don't "ignite," "unlock," "spark," "unleash" (real humans don't speak this way)
- Reassurance sentences — the BIG IDEA isn't emotional comfort, it's a creative platform
</avoid_this>

<examples_to_emulate>
For style, not content:
- "Make it doable."
- "Start small. Go far."
- "Less hassle. More happening."
- "Make it feel possible."
- "Small step. Big shift."
- "Do it without the drama."
- "Keep it simple. Make it work."
- "Start now, stress less."
- "More living, less waiting."

Short. Human. Useful. Campaignable.
</examples_to_emulate>

</human_language_principles>
